YOUR ULTIMATE BEACH BUCKETLIST

Created by actual beach lovers and true passionate travel professionals, our definitive, thorough and illuminating lists provide travelers with a one-stop resource for the most enchanting and exotic shores in the world.

Trust us, you do not need to find any other list! This is THE most comprehensive guide ever made, and it is sure to ignite your wanderlust and inspire the planning of your next great beach adventure.

So, why is our lists unlike any other? Because we crafted The World’s 50 Best Beaches, by conferring with over 1200 journalists, editors, bloggers, influencers and agencies, who possess endless travel wisdom. These are the expert, the ones who have traveled the world, had their feet in the sand and dipped in the seas. And they helped us create a list of beaches that will aide all other travelers and beach lovers out there access shores that will leave a magical and indelible imprint.

For the voting process we asked these 1200 travel professionals to nominate and vote for the beaches they believe deserve the title of the very best in the world. And can we just say, they brought incredible insight! Upon completion of the voting process, our in-house travel experts analyzed and categorized the results for the final lists. Also, we made sure to add all the important information about these specific beaches so travelers out there can find the right time to go, how to get there and learn why this particular beach deserved a spot on the list.

And we didn't just make one list for you. No, we created three official lists, The World's 50 Best Beaches, Europe's 50 Best Beaches and North America's 50 Best Beaches. In the coming years we aspire to add even more to the list such as the best beaches in South America, Central America and the Caribbean, Australia and the South Pacific and Asia. Even further, we want to rank the top 50 beaches within or near cities and the top 50 most remote, or untouched, beaches.

Our goal is to continously provide you with the best and most updated insight into what beaches you simply have to put on your bucketlist!

So, pack your bags and get ready to sink your toes into the white sand, relax under the swaying palm trees, soak in the turquoise waters, explore volcanic coastlines, and gaze at snow-capped mountain peaks steps from the sea. The resulting lists are a compelling collection of life-changing experiences.
